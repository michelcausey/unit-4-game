Crystal Game

Instructions:

1. You will be given a random number at the start of the game.

2. Each of the four crystals have a different value, which will be added to your total score.

3. The value of each crystal is hidden until you click on it.

To win the game: match your total score to the indicated Target Number.

You lose if: your total score goes above the target number.

At the end of every game, the value of each crystal will change.
